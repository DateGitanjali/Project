AI Tools

ChatGPT

EDeveloped by OpenAI, ChatGPT is a conversational AI model capable of answering questions, writing content, coding, tutoring, and more, based on the GPT architecture.

Gemini

Built by Google DeepMind, Gemini is a multimodal AI model that can process text, images, and more.

Copilot

Created by GitHub in collaboration with OpenAI, Copilot is an AI coding assistant that helps developers by suggesting code snippets, completing functions, and reducing development time.

Claude

Developed by Anthropic, Claude is a family of helpful, safe, and controllable AI assistants focused on ethical use, capable of long-form content generation, Q&A, and task automation.

AI Models

GPT-4

Developed by OpenAI, GPT-4 is a powerful language model known for advanced reasoning, coding, and content generation.

BERT

Created by Google, BERT understands the context of words in a sentence, enabling better language understanding for search and NLP.

Transformer

Introduced by Google, Transformer is a neural network architecture that powers modern NLP models like GPT and BERT.

PaLM

PaLM (Pathways Language Model) by Google is designed for complex reasoning, coding, and multilingual understanding.
